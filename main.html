Exploring Paradise: Maldives

The Maldives, a tropical paradise in the Indian Ocean, is a dream destination for beach lovers and water enthusiasts.


Top 5 Attractions

  1. Crystal-clear waters for snorkeling and diving
  2. Overwater bungalows with stunning ocean views
  3. White sandy beaches perfect for relaxation
  4. Rich marine life and colorful coral reefs
  5. Spectacular sunsets on the horizon

Must-Try Foods

  • Hedhika: Traditional Maldivian snacks
  • Garudhiya: Flavorful fish soup
  • Mas Huni: Tuna salad with coconut
  • Bis Keemiya: Pastry filled with fish, coconut, and spices
  • Rihaakuru: Thick fish paste, often eaten with rice
